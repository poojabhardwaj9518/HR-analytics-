# HR Analytics Project

An end-to-end HR Analytics project designed to track key workforce metrics, analyze employee turnover, and uncover insights to improve retention, optimize employee satisfaction, and drive organizational performance using data visualization.

---

## 📊 Business Overview & Objectives
The primary goal of this project is to provide HR department heads and executive management with a data-driven overview of employee demographics, attrition factors, and overall workforce health.

### Key KPIs Tracked:
* **Total Employees:** Overall headcount within the organization.
* **Attrition Count & Rate:** The number and percentage of employees who have left the company.
* **Active Employees:** Current workforce count available for operations.
* **Average Age & Tenure:** Demographics tracking to understand workforce experience and life cycle.

## 📈 Dashboard Insights & Features
The analytics dashboard provides critical visual breakdowns across several key workplace dimensions:

* **Attrition by Department & Role:** Pinpointing which segments (e.g., Sales, R&D, HR) experience the highest turnover rates.
* **Demographic Analysis:** Tracking employee distributions across age groups, gender, and education fields.
* **Job Satisfaction Metrics:** Mapping how factors like relationship satisfaction, environment satisfaction, and work-life balance correlate with employee retention.
* **Tenure & Income Factors:** Evaluating the impact of monthly income levels and years at the company on attrition.
